VariantVault — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

VariantVault is a Chrome extension that helps users view Shopify product variant information on Shopify-powered websites. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data VariantVault accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Data Collection

VariantVault does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, cookies, or any third-party data services.

Data Accessed Locally

To function, the extension reads publicly available product information from Shopify-powered web pages you visit. Specifically:

  • Product variant data — variant IDs, titles, prices, and availability displayed on the current page.
  • Page content — the extension reads the current page's HTML and embedded script data to extract product information.
  • Monitored URLs — if you use the stock monitor feature, the URLs you choose to monitor are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API.

All of this data stays entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to any external server owned or operated by VariantVault.

Network Requests

The extension may make network requests to the Shopify store you are browsing (e.g., fetching product JSON endpoints or RSC data) in order to retrieve variant information. These requests go directly to the store's servers — not to any VariantVault server. The stock monitor feature periodically checks product URLs you have opted to monitor.

Permissions

VariantVault requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — to read product data from the page you are currently viewing when you click the extension icon.
  • scripting — to inject a content script into the active tab that extracts product variant information from the page.
  • storage — to save your monitored URLs and extension preferences locally on your device.
  • alarms — to periodically check monitored product URLs for stock availability.
  • notifications — to alert you when a monitored product becomes available.

Third-Party Services

VariantVault does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Data Storage & Retention

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension or clearing extension data in Chrome settings. No data persists on any external server.

Children's Privacy

VariantVault is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
